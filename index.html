Life at Just Eat

Right now, Just Eat is an exciting place to be. We're growing rapidly and we want to continue to create wow experiences and make them even more delightful for our restaurant partners and customers. After all, that's exactly why we do what we do!

World party

The cherry on our cake is the annual World Party - an epic bash that brings together over 1,000 members of the Just Eat family from all corners of the globe

Friday sundowners

Friday means drinks, snacks and socialising at Sundowners. We ring a bell at 4:30 and everyone has a chance to unwind and mingle!

Summer party

Every year we have a different-themed summer party, last year it was called the Jamboree where Just Eaters took part in an 'It's a Knockout' style competition with a big party to follow!

Christmas party

Oh, and there’s our legendary themed Christmas parties later in the year too.

Our values

We hire grads because we want our senior roles to be handed down to people who have grown in this company. We're proud of what we've built, and the countless ways we've connected people with food, everywhere.

Our graduate location

Our graduate offering is expanding just as we are! we now have graduate programmes in the UK and Madrid as well as local, dedicated customer care teams in every country we operate in. As graduates you will be influencing both global and local business. Not many graduates can say that!
